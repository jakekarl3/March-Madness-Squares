Rules

Move over Super Bowl, March Madness is upon us. Using the same concept as Super Bowl squares, a 10x10 grid of boxes is set up and each row and column are assigned a number from 0 to 9 at random. Numbers will be assigned after all 100 squares have been assigned. Each square will be purchased for $10, a maximum of 5 squares per person.

Winning boxes are determined by looking at the last digits of the final score of each game. The top row of numbers (horizontal) represents the winning team while the side (vertical) represents the losing team.

For example, the final score of the 2022 March Madness championship was Kansas 72 UNC 69. The box corresponding to the 2 column and 9 row would be the winner.

63 total games will be scored starting with the round of 64. Payouts for a winning score will start at $5 in the round of 64 and increase as the tournament goes on, listed below. Winnings will be tabulated as the games finish and all payouts will be made after the final game is scored.

All payments can be made to Jake Karl's venmo, @jakekarl3. Let me know which squares you would like, otherwise I will pick at random for you. If you have any questions feel free to send me a text at 630-730-7041

Payouts

  • Round of 64 (32 games): $5/game
  • Round of 32 (16 games): $10/game
  • Sweet 16 (8 games): $20/game
  • Elite 8 (4 games): $40/game
  • Final 4 (2 games): $80/game
  • Championship (1 game): $200/game
Buy-in: $10/square (5 squares max)
